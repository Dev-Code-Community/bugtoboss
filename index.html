Who can participate?
Anyone passionate about technology can participate! Whether you're a student, professional, or self-taught developer, BugToBoss welcomes all skill levels.
What types of projects are accepted?
We accept projects from various domains including web development, mobile apps, AI/ML, blockchain, IoT, and more. The project should be original and created by you.
How are projects evaluated?
Projects are evaluated based on innovation, technical complexity, social impact, and community engagement. Our panel of industry experts will review each submission.
